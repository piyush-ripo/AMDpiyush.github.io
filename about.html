About AMD

Advanced Micro Devices, Inc. (AMD) is a global semiconductor company that designs and manufactures a range of microprocessors and other computing and graphics hardware. Founded in 1969, AMD is headquartered in Santa Clara, California and has operations around the world.

Our Products

AMD offers a wide range of microprocessors for desktop and laptop computers, including its popular Ryzen and Athlon lines. In addition to CPUs, AMD also manufactures graphics processing units (GPUs) and other hardware for gaming, data center, and professional markets.

Our Values

At AMD, we are committed to innovation and excellence. We strive to deliver the best possible products and experiences to our customers, and we believe in the power of technology to make the world a better place. Our values of integrity, accountability, and respect drive everything we do.

AMD processors offer excellent performance and value for money. With a strong focus on innovation and customer satisfaction, AMD is a trusted choice for consumers and businesses around the world.
